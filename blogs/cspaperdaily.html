Introducing CS Paper Daily

2/18/2025

The old saying goes "An apple a day keeps the doctor away.” For people like me, "A CS paper a day keeps the bugs at bay" sounds better. As a developer, I want to expose myself to new ideas constantly. That being said, there’s an overwhelming amount of resources out there making it intimidating to start.

So, I created the site cspaperdaily.com. It gives users computer science papers to read each day with the goal of creating a habit.
