Introducing Cold Recruit

10/30/2025


Last year, I realized just how broken the job seeking process is. I was a CS senior at Vanderbilt looking for a full time job, and despite good grades, internships, and personal projects, I realized applying straight to open positions online is a hard way to get a job. With that struggle came a realization; the best way to get a job is to know people.

So, I made Cold Recruit, an AI networking tool to help send great cold messages.

Based on your information and your target’s LinkedIn posts, you can get a personalized message to send within seconds. If you’re job hunting, networking, etc, this is a great tool. The video is not sped up, it's that quick.

- Marius
