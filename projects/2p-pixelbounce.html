  • 2p Pixel Bounce App

    A very fun two player Unity app. You try to shoot little pixelated bullets and try to hit the other person on the other side. I have a really fond memory of connecting with my younger cousins that I hadn't seen in 5 years with this game. This was my first project in Unity that used animations.
