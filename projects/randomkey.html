  • Random Key App

    A Unity app designed to give me a random music key to play in. Just tap a button and get a music key. I made it because I tend to play piano mostly in basic keys. When I play I do improv or play a song I know by ear, so it's usually up to me what key to play in, so I've used this app quite a bit to mix things up. I created this on a Saturday morning because I didn't want study.
