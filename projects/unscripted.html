  • Unscripted

    I was inspired after watching Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse. Two of my favorite parts visually are the expectations drawing and falling upside down. So, I decided to do something similar in Adobe Illustrator, but instead of "no expectations", I put "unscripted" as I feel that is a motto that I vibe with more. From there, I replaced the ouline of Miles in the original to an outline of him falling, and then made the background the buildings from the falling scene as well.

    Finished: 5/11/2020
